# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our Android applications (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you explicitly provide it to us (for example, by contacting support).

### Automatically Collected Information
When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:
- Device information (e.g., model, operating system version)
- Usage data (e.g., features used, time spent in the app)
- Crash reports and performance data

This information is collected anonymously and is used solely to improve the performance and stability of the App.

### Permissions
The App may request certain permissions on your device (e.g., Camera, Storage, Location) to provide core functionalities. You can enable or disable these permissions at any time through your device settings.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- To provide, maintain, and improve the App
- To troubleshoot bugs and analyze performance
- To respond to your questions or support requests

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you. These services may include:
- Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics for Firebase)
- Crash reporting tools (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics)
- Advertising networks (if applicable)

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party service providers to understand their data practices.

## 4. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us with your information, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 5. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to take necessary actions.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
